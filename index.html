About Me

Hi there, and welcome to my creative portfolio. While I took some time off from these things, I began this journey back in 2021. I am well versed in video editing and shooting. I also thrive at content creation and vertical video, which I have been doing since 2021, working with the University of Pittsburgh Gymnastics team, and the Univeristy of Pittsburg's SAAC. While not only does this website showcase my creative skills, I also built this website from the base up using HTML,CSS and Bootstrap.

Photos

I took these images with a Canon XS Rebel 7 using a 18mm-50mm lense. I have a passion for showing off the beauty that is in the simpliest things, which is why all of these photos are stripped down to just the subject(s) and who they are as people. To edit all of my photos I use adobe Lightroom, these photos only took simple light touches, no major adjustments.
